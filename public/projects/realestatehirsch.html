Marketing Website

RealEstateHirsch

A responsive leasing hub for a family-run business. I designed and engineered every page to guide visitors from property storytelling into tour bookings, confirmation emails, and leasing workflows.

Role
Product Designer · Frontend Developer
Timeline
Winter 2023 · 6 Weeks
Stack
HTML · CSS · JavaScript · Express.js · MySQL · Nodemailer · Flatpickr
Result
Live on Render with automated booking & email confirmations

What the site delivers

RealEstateHirsch turns a family leasing business into a guided digital experience that keeps prospects moving from discovery to tour confirmation.

  • Responsive landing pages that showcase listings and keep scheduling calls-to-action above the fold.
  • Interactive tour scheduling with live availability, server-side validation, and automated confirmation emails.
  • Reusable content components so agents can refresh listings, availability, and promotions without touching code.

My role

  • Led product design, copy, and brand styling for the entire site.
  • Built the Express.js backend powering scheduling, email automation, and MySQL storage.
  • Set up deployment on Render and created quick-edit documentation for the team.

Outcomes & next steps

  • Automated confirmations ensure new leads receive same-day responses without manual follow-up.
  • The guided scheduler gives prospects a clear path from browsing to booking a tour.
  • Reusable content modules make it easy for agents to update availability without touching code.

Next

Roadmap items include integrating the contact form into the email workflow, adding a lightweight CMS for seasonal promotions, and exploring subtle WebGL flourishes that preserve current Lighthouse scores.
