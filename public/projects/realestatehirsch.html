Marketing Website

RealEstateHirsch

Responsive leasing site that guides visitors from browsing to tour confirmation.

I designed and built the full experience so this family business could showcase listings, capture leads, and automate confirmations without extra tools.

Core experience

Built to keep prospects moving toward a confirmed tour.

Story-driven landing

Hero imagery, testimonials, and quick stats establish trust while keeping the primary tour CTA in view across breakpoints.

Interactive scheduling

Flatpickr calendar, live availability, and server-side validation prevent double bookings and confirm visits instantly.

Easy updates

Reusable content modules let agents refresh availability and promotions without editing code.

Under the hood

Lightweight backend services keep the team responsive.

Express API

Validates requests, stores reservations in MySQL, and protects against overlapping date/time combinations.

Email automation

Nodemailer templates send confirmations to both visitors and the leasing inbox with reschedule/cancel links.

Self-service portal

Unique cancel IDs let guests manage their reservation directly from their confirmation email.
