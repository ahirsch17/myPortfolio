Schedule Intelligence

SamePath

Solo-built mobile app that finds shared free time so classmates can link up fast.

SamePath turns course lists into smart overlap suggestions. Students import schedules, approve friends, and get quick nudges when they share a class or a long break.

Why it matters

Students wanted quick ways to see who shares their classes without exposing their entire schedule.

Schedule import

Students paste CRNs or upload their SIS export; SamePath normalizes the data and maps it to meeting times.

Overlap scoring

A TypeScript service ranks shared breaks by length, proximity, and user preferences so the best windows surface first.

Privacy on demand

Visibility only flips on after a mutual connection. Anyone can pause sharing, remove a friend, or hide specific days with one tap.

How it works

Lightweight architecture keeps the app fast for beta testers and easy to extend.

Client

React Native app handles schedule import, friend approvals, and notifications. Local caching keeps critical data offline.

API

Flask endpoints clean and store schedule data, calculate overlaps on demand, and respect per-user visibility rules.

Storage

Azure Table Storage keeps student schedules and friendships lightweight while avoiding the overhead of a relational database.

Next steps

Beta feedback is guiding polish before a wider campus rollout.

  • Layering in calendar analytics for mentors and student success teams.
  • Hardening ARIA patterns and keyboard support for accessibility audits.
  • Preparing the matching engine to support multiple campuses.
