Schedule Intelligence

SamePath

SamePath is a solo-built project that unlocks shared time for classmates. I designed and engineered the system—from the concept to the APIs—to help students see who shares their courses, where long breaks overlap, and when it’s worth catching a friend between classes.

Role
Founder · Product Designer · Full Stack Engineer
Timeline
Spring 2024 · 10 Weeks
Stack
React Native · TypeScript · Flask · Azure Table Storage
Status
Private Beta · On-campus pilots

What the app delivers

SamePath keeps classmates aware of shared schedules so planning study time feels effortless and respectful of privacy.

  • Students import or search their class schedule, then see overlapping breaks and classes with approved friends.
  • Compatibility scoring highlights the best meet-up windows first, so planning takes a few taps.
  • Privacy tools let anyone pause sharing, remove connections, or hide specific days at any time.

My role

  • Designed the product flows, prototypes, and visual language.
  • Developed the React Native client and Flask APIs with Azure storage.
  • Led usability sessions to refine onboarding and privacy messaging.

Outcomes & next steps

  • Usability sessions confirmed schedule explanations help classmates understand why a connection surfaced.
  • Push notifications nudge study groups to reconnect during busy weeks like midterms.
  • Pilots with commuters explore how to use shared breaks to stay on campus between classes.

Next

I’m layering in analytics dashboards for mentors, iterating on ARIA patterns for accessibility, and preparing the matching service for more universities.
